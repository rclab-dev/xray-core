xray-core — Skin editor

Pick colors & process order · export / import · bring & share your own.
A skin is the look of X-Ray — colors and the process stacking order. The meaning (Overview = an x-ray of the topology, DeepDive = inside the router, and the data contract) is fixed; the look is yours. Edit below, Export the JSON, and hand it to anyone. Your choice is saved in this browser and every gallery demo picks it up. Frozen shape: SKIN-CONTRACT.md. Phase 1 = colors (process order & shapes come in Phase 2).
